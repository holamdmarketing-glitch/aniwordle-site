Anime Higher or Lower

Two anime characters, one question: which one is more popular? Pick more or fewer favourites and keep your streak alive as long as you can. No account, no sign-up, just the game.

How to play

You are shown two anime characters. The first reveals its number of fan favourites; for the second, you decide whether it has more or fewer. Get it right and your score climbs while a fresh character slides in to face the previous winner. Get it wrong and the run ends, so every call counts.

Beat your best streak

Higher or Lower is an endless game: there is no six-guess limit and no daily reset, just how far you can push your streak in a single run. Your best score is saved in your browser, and you can share it to dare your friends to beat it.

Tips

  • Main characters from long-running shounen usually rack up huge favourite counts.
  • A beloved side character can still outscore the lead of a niche series, do not assume.
  • When two feel close, lean on which fandom is simply bigger overall.

Frequently asked questions

How do you play Anime Higher or Lower?+

You see two anime characters. One shows its number of fan favourites; for the other, you decide whether it has more or fewer. Guess right and your streak grows and a new character appears. One wrong answer ends the run.

What does "favourites" mean?+

Favourites are how many fans have added that character to their favourites on public anime databases. It is a simple, widely used proxy for how popular a character is.

Is there a daily puzzle?+

Higher or Lower is an endless high-score game rather than a single daily puzzle: play as many rounds as you can in one run. Your best streak is saved in your browser, and you can share your score to challenge friends.

Which characters can appear?+

Characters are drawn from a curated pool of 364 popular characters across 83 anime, so the match-ups stay recognisable.

Do I need an account?+

No. The game runs in your browser and your best streak is stored locally on your device. No sign-up, no ads in the way.
