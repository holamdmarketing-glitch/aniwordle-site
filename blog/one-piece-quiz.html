One Piece Quiz: 20 Trivia Questions for Real Fans

One Piece is the best-selling manga of all time, and after more than a thousand chapters the lore runs deep. This quiz is a fair test: it starts with questions any anime fan can answer and works up to the kind of trivia only a real nakama remembers. Keep score as you go, no peeking at the wiki, and meet us at the bottom for the daily One Piece character game.

Easy: East Blue warm-up

These should be free points if you have watched the first arc.

  1. What is the name of Luffy’s signature attack, the one he shouts before throwing a stretched punch?
  2. Which Devil Fruit did Luffy eat, and what type is it (Paramecia, Zoan or Logia)?
  3. Who is the first member to join the Straw Hat crew after Luffy?
  4. What is the dream that Zoro is chasing?
  5. What food is Luffy most obsessed with throughout the series?

Medium: Grand Line knowledge

Now it gets interesting. These reward fans who have followed the crew past Alabasta.

  1. What is the name of Nami’s home village, and who occupied it for years?
  2. Which Straw Hat has a bounty that was originally only 30 Berries, the lowest in the crew at the time?
  3. What is the name of Sanji’s signature fighting style that uses only his legs?
  4. Who taught Luffy the difference between the three types of Haki?
  5. What is the name of the ship the crew sails after the Going Merry?

Hard: New World deep cuts

This is where casual viewers fall off. Only real fans clear this block.

  1. What are the four Road Poneglyphs said to reveal when their locations are combined?
  2. Which Yonko did Luffy defeat to earn the title of the Fifth Emperor in the eyes of the World Government?
  3. What is the true name of the Gum-Gum Fruit, revealed during the Wano arc?
  4. Who was the previous owner of Zoro’s cursed blade Sandai Kitetsu before he claimed it in Loguetown?
  5. What is the name of Trafalgar Law’s Devil Fruit, and what does its “Room” let him do?

Expert: only legends pass

If you get all five of these, you have earned your pirate flag.

  1. What is the name of the ancient weapon tied to the mermaid Princess Shirahoshi?
  2. Which character is revealed to have eaten the Mythical Zoan model that turns them into a being from Wano legend?
  3. What was the original crew name of the pirate who left the One Piece treasure on Laugh Tale?
  4. How many years passed during the timeskip after the Marineford war?
  5. What three words did Gol D. Roger say that launched the Great Pirate Era?

How did you score?

  • 0 to 7: A solid start. You know the crew, now go fill in the Grand Line.
  • 8 to 14: A proper fan. You have done the long arcs and it shows.
  • 15 to 20: Pirate King material. Very little about this series gets past you.
