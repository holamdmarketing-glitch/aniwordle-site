The Most Popular Female Anime Characters, Ranked by Fan Votes

Every "best female anime characters" list online is somebody's personal opinion or a crowd vote with no numbers attached. This one is different. The order below comes straight from AniList favourite counts, the closest thing the anime community has to a global ballot, so it is the fandom speaking, not one writer. The result is a mix you would expect and a few surprises: a quiet half-elf beats out a fan-favourite demon slayer, and an antagonist cracks the top five. Here are the fifteen women anime fans rally behind the most, with a note on why each one earned it.

1

#1 Emilia

Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- · 28,241 favourites

Emilia carries Re:Zero as the half-elf Subaru keeps dying to protect, and she earns the top spot without ever needing to be the loudest person in the room. Her quiet kindness in a story built on cruelty is exactly what fans latched onto, and the silver hair and spirit magic do not hurt either.

2

#2 Mikasa Ackerman

Attack on Titan · 27,828 favourites

Mikasa is the most capable soldier in Attack on Titan and arguably its emotional core. Her devotion to Eren reads as either heartbreaking or frustrating depending on the day, but nobody questions the skill: she takes down titans most of the Survey Corps would run from.

3

#3 Kurisu Makise

Steins;Gate · 22,602 favourites

Kurisu is the genius neuroscientist who makes Steins;Gate tick, equal parts sharp tongue and buried warmth. Fans love that the romance is built on actual intellectual sparring, and "Christina" remains one of the best-written tsunderes in anime.

4

#4 Makima

Chainsaw Man · 22,470 favourites

Makima is the rare antagonist who tops a popularity list. Calm, controlling and genuinely unsettling, she turned Chainsaw Man into a study of devotion and power. You are not supposed to trust her, and that is the whole appeal.

5

#5 Mai Sakurajima

Rascal Does Not Dream of Bunny Girl Senpai · 22,152 favourites

Mai is the upperclassman at the heart of Bunny Girl Senpai, and her grounded, dry wit set her apart from the usual love interest. The chemistry with Sakuta feels earned, which is why she landed so hard with viewers.

6

#6 Frieren

Frieren: Beyond Journey’s End · 21,748 favourites

Frieren is a recent arrival who shot straight up the rankings. An elf mage processing a thousand years of grief at human speed gave the fandom something genuinely new, and the show wrapped it in some of the best animation of its year.

7

#7 Kaguya Shinomiya

Kaguya-sama: Love is War · 21,247 favourites

Kaguya runs the most beloved romcom mind-games in modern anime. Watching a proud, brilliant student council vice president completely lose her composure over a crush is the joke that powers the whole series.

8

#8 Violet Evergarden

Violet Evergarden · 20,952 favourites

Violet is the former child soldier learning what feelings are by ghost-writing letters for other people. Few characters earn their tears as honestly, and Violet Evergarden is half art exhibit, half emotional gut-punch because of her.

9

#9 Power

Chainsaw Man · 20,519 favourites

Power is loud, selfish, greedy and somehow impossible to dislike. The Blood Fiend gave Chainsaw Man its comic engine, and her bond with Denji turns into one of the most quietly devastating relationships in the show.

10

#10 Megumin

KONOSUBA -God's blessing on this wonderful world! · 17,219 favourites

Megumin turned a one-note gag, an explosion-obsessed mage who can only cast a single spell per day, into a fandom phenomenon. KonoSuba lives on its cast, and she is the one people quote.

11

#11 Chika Fujiwara

Kaguya-sama: Love is War · 16,933 favourites

Chika is the chaos agent of Kaguya-sama, the airheaded wildcard who derails every scheme. The viral dance and her sheer unpredictability made her a fan favourite well beyond the show itself.

12

#12 Marin Kitagawa

My Dress-Up Darling · 16,901 favourites

Marin is the gyaru who pulls a shy doll-maker into cosplay, and her open, judgement-free enthusiasm made My Dress-Up Darling a breakout. She is popular because she is genuinely nice, a refreshing change of pace.

13

#13 Rem

Re:ZERO -Starting Life in Another World- · 16,749 favourites

Rem is the Re:Zero maid whose arc became one of anime's most cited fan moments. Her devotion to Subaru after everything resonated so hard that "Rem won the popularity poll even if she lost the plot" became a running joke.

14

#14 Ichigo

DARLING in the FRANXX · 16,305 favourites

Ichigo is the squad leader in Darling in the Franxx whose unrequited feelings made her one of the show's most debated characters. Fans split hard over her, and strong opinions tend to mean high engagement.

15

#15 Nezuko Kamado

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba · 16,099 favourites

Nezuko is the demon little sister Tanjiro refuses to give up on, and she became Demon Slayer's mascot almost overnight. The bamboo muzzle and the fierce protectiveness made her instantly iconic.

Frequently asked questions

Who is the most popular female anime character?+

By AniList fan favourites, Emilia from Re:Zero and Mikasa Ackerman from Attack on Titan sit at the very top, with Kurisu Makise (Steins;Gate) and Makima (Chainsaw Man) close behind. This list is ranked by those real favourite counts.

Who is the number one waifu in anime?+

It depends on the poll, but Rem from Re:Zero is the perennial answer, famous for "winning" popularity contests even when she loses the story. By raw favourites across all of anime, Emilia and Mikasa currently edge ahead.

How is this ranking decided?+

The order comes from favourite counts on AniList, a large public anime database where fans tag the characters they love. It reflects community sentiment rather than a single editor's pick, and the commentary explains why each one resonates.
