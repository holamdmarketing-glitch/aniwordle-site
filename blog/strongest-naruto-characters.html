The Strongest Naruto Characters, Ranked by the Fans

Arguing about the strongest Naruto characters is a rite of passage for any fan, and the answer shifts depending on whether you weigh raw power, feats, or the moments that stuck with you. So instead of one more personal opinion, this ranking is ordered by the hard numbers: how many fans have favourited each character on AniList, the largest open anime database. It is the closest thing to a community vote we have. Below each name we break down why that character earns the spot on power and feats. Read the list, then jump into the daily Naruto character quiz at the bottom and see how many you can recognise.

1

#1 Itachi Uchiha

Naruto · 22,572 favourites

Itachi tops nearly every fan poll for a reason: he wiped out his own clan in a single night, held his own against Sasuke while terminally ill and half-blind, and turned out to be the most selfless character in the series. The Sharingan, Susanoo and Izanami make him a tactical nightmare even from beyond the grave.

2

#2 Kakashi Hatake

Naruto · 22,091 favourites

Kakashi reads opponents faster than they can move. The Copy Ninja learned a thousand jutsu with his transplanted Sharingan, and his Kamui lets him phase attacks into another dimension. As Sixth Hokage he is the benchmark every younger shinobi measures themselves against.

3

#3 Naruto Uzumaki

Naruto · 19,412 favourites

The title character grows from dead-last prankster to the strongest shinobi alive. Sage Mode, Kurama and the Six Paths chakra stack into raw power nobody else touches, but it is his refusal to give up on anyone that actually wins his fights.

4

#4 Sasuke Uchiha

Naruto · 13,818 favourites

Sasuke is the only character who fights Naruto to a standstill twice. The Mangekyo Sharingan, Rinnegan and Indra-blessed lightning make him the perfect dark mirror: every gain in power costs him something, which is exactly why fans cannot look away.

5

#5 Shikamaru Nara

Naruto · 8,725 favourites

Shikamaru never has the strongest jutsu in the room and wins anyway. His Shadow Possession plus an IQ over 200 let him out-think enemies who massively outclass him on paper, including the kill that avenged Asuma.

6

#6 Gaara

Naruto · 7,854 favourites

Gaara goes from unhinged jinchuriki to the most respected Kazekage in living memory. His absolute sand defence almost never breaks, and his arc from feared monster to trusted leader is one of the cleanest redemptions in the franchise.

7

#7 Jiraiya

Naruto · 6,601 favourites

Jiraiya is a Sannin, Sage Mode pioneer and the teacher who shaped both Naruto and the Fourth Hokage. He fought Pain to the edge of victory while outnumbered six to one, and his death is the gut punch the whole war arc is built on.

8

#8 Hinata Hyuuga

Naruto · 6,268 favourites

Hinata pairs the Hyuga Gentle Fist with a courage the early series never gave her credit for. Standing alone in front of Pain to protect Naruto is still one of the bravest moments in the show, and her Byakugan makes her a genuine front-line threat.

9

#9 Pain

Naruto · 5,438 favourites

As the face of Pain, Nagato flattens the entire Hidden Leaf with the Six Paths and the Almighty Push. The Rinnegan puts him in god tier, and his philosophy on pain and peace gives the series its heaviest argument.

10

#10 Rock Lee

Naruto · 5,370 favourites

Rock Lee cannot use ninjutsu at all and still earns his spot through pure taijutsu. The Eight Gates turn him into a momentary monster who pushed Gaara to the brink, proof that hard work alone can stand beside genius in this world.

11

#11 Sakura Haruno

Naruto · 2,963 favourites

Sakura trained under Tsunade into a medical prodigy whose chakra-enhanced strength shatters the ground. By the war she is healing armies and trading blows with goddess-tier enemies, a long way from the genin she started as.

12

#12 Neji Hyuuga

Naruto · 2,707 favourites

Neji was the Hyuga prodigy who could close every chakra point in your body before you landed a hit. His Eight Trigrams Sixty-Four Palms is iconic, and his final act protecting Naruto and Hinata cements him as more than the rival he began as.

Frequently asked questions

Who is the strongest Naruto character?+

By raw end-of-series power, Naruto Uzumaki and Sasuke Uchiha sit at the top thanks to Six Paths chakra and the Rinnegan. By fan popularity, Itachi Uchiha consistently ranks first. This list is ordered by fan favourites, with power and feats explained for each.

How is this ranking decided?+

The order comes from real favourite counts on AniList, a large public anime database, so it reflects community sentiment rather than a single writer's opinion. The commentary then explains each character's strength and key feats.
