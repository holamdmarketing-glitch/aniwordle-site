What Is Animedle? The Anime Wordle Explained

If you have seen the word “Animedle” in an anime group chat and were not sure what it meant, the short answer is: it is Wordle, but for anime. This guide explains the format, where the name comes from, and how to start playing today.

Where the name comes from

In late 2021, Wordle turned a simple once-a-day word puzzle into a global habit. The format was sticky for three reasons: everyone got the same puzzle each day, you could only play once, and the result was easy to share without spoilers. Fans quickly built spin-offs for every hobby, and they all borrowed the “-dle” suffix. Animedle is the anime branch of that family: “anime” plus “-dle”.

How an Animedle works

The core loop is always the same:

  • There is one hidden answer per day, usually an anime character or a series.
  • You make a guess, and the game gives you feedback that narrows the field.
  • The feedback is what makes it more than luck. Guess a character and the game tells you which traits match the secret one: gender, the anime they are from, their role, the year they debuted, and so on. Green means an exact match, so each guess is a clue toward the next.
  • Everyone sees the same answer that day, which is what makes scores worth comparing.

It rewards two things at once: how much anime you have watched, and how well you reason from partial information.

Animedle vs other anime guessing games

Not every anime guessing game is an Animedle. The label specifically means the daily, attribute-clue, Wordle-style format. A few neighbours:

  • Guess the Character shows a blurred portrait that sharpens with each wrong guess. It is daily and shareable, but the clue is the image, not a grid of attributes.
  • Guess the Anime does the same with a pixelated scene from a series.
  • Higher or Lower asks which of two characters is more popular, endlessly, with no daily limit.

The Animedle is the one built directly on the Wordle attribute grid.

Frequently asked questions

What does Animedle mean?+

Animedle is a blend of 'anime' and the '-dle' suffix popularised by Wordle. It refers to a daily anime guessing game where everyone gets the same puzzle each day and clues narrow down the answer.

Is Animedle the same as Anime Wordle?+

They are used interchangeably. Both describe a once-a-day anime guessing game in the Wordle format. On Aniwordle, the Anime Wordle game is the Animedle: guess the daily character and each guess reveals matching attributes.

Is Animedle free to play?+

Yes. Animedle-style games are free and need no account. Aniwordle saves your streak and stats in your browser.
