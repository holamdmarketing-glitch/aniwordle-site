Anime Wordle (Animedle)

Guess today's mystery anime character. Every guess reveals how its attributes line up with the answer, anime, gender, role, genres, debut year and popularity. New animedle every day.

How the anime wordle works

Type any anime character and lock in your guess. A row appears comparing six attributes against the hidden answer. A green cell is an exact match, yellow is close, andred is a miss. Arrows on debut year and popularity point you toward the answer. You have six guesses to turn every cell green.

One animedle a day

The mystery character is shared by every player and resets at midnight UTC, so you and your friends are always solving the same puzzle. Fewer guesses is a better score. Finished early? Practice mode serves unlimited random characters to sharpen your knowledge.

Strategy tips

  • Open with a popular character from a big franchise to light up several attributes at once.
  • Use genre overlaps (yellow) to jump between series that share a tone.
  • Follow the debut-year and popularity arrows to triangulate the era and fame of the answer.

Frequently asked questions

What is Anime Wordle?+

Anime Wordle (also called animedle) is a daily guessing game where you identify a mystery anime character. After each guess, a grid shows how that character compares to the answer, green for a match, yellow for close, red for no match, until you find the right one.

What do the colours and arrows mean?+

Green means that attribute (anime, gender, role, genres, debut year or popularity) exactly matches the answer. Yellow means it is close, a partial genre overlap, a debut year within three years, or similar popularity. Red means no match. An up or down arrow on debut year and popularity tells you the answer is higher or lower.

How many guesses do I get?+

You get six guesses. Use the colour-coded clues to close in on the daily answer; if you run out, the mystery character is revealed. Your score is how few guesses it took, share it and compare with friends.

Is it the same puzzle for everyone?+

Yes. The mystery character is the same for all players each day and changes at midnight UTC. Want more? Practice mode gives you unlimited random rounds.

Which characters can appear?+

The answer is drawn from 364 popular characters across 83 anime, ranked by fan popularity so every puzzle is solvable.
