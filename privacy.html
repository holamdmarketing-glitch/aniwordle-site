Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-23

Game data

Aniwordle stores your game progress, streaks and statistics locally in your browser usinglocalStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us or any third party. Clearing your browser storage will reset it.

Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous usage (such as which games are most played). This does not identify you personally.

Cookies & advertising

The site currently runs without advertising. If display advertising is introduced in the future, this policy will be updated to describe any advertising cookies and your choices.

Contact

For any privacy questions or rights-holder concerns, please get in touch.
