Guess the Anime Character

A blurred anime character is revealed bit by bit. Name them before you run out of guesses, a new mystery character every day, with hints along the way. No account, no ads in your way, just the game.

How to play

Look at the blurred portrait and type the name of the anime character you think it is. As you type, matching characters appear, pick one to lock in your guess. Get it wrong and the image becomes a little clearer while a new clue is revealed: first their gender, then the year their anime debuted, then their role, and finally the anime they are from. You have six guesses.

A new character every day

Like Wordle, everyone sees the same character each day, so you can compare scores with friends and the wider anime community. Solve it in fewer guesses to climb your streak. When you finish, share your result, the spoiler-free emoji grid shows how many tries it took without giving the answer away.

Tips for guessing anime characters

  • Start broad: silhouettes and hair colour are often visible even through the blur.
  • Use the gender and debut-year hints to narrow down the era and series.
  • Save a guess, the anime hint on your fourth miss usually gives it away.
  • Out of ideas? Switch to Practice mode and learn new characters with unlimited rounds.

Frequently asked questions

How do you play Guess the Anime Character?+

A blurred anime character image appears. Type a character name and pick from the suggestions. Each wrong guess slightly unblurs the picture and reveals a new hint (gender, debut year, role, then the anime). You have 6 guesses to name them.

Is there a new character every day?+

Yes. Everyone gets the same mystery character each day, chosen deterministically by date, so you can compare results with friends. A new character unlocks at midnight UTC. Practice mode lets you play unlimited random characters anytime.

Where do the characters come from?+

Characters are drawn from a curated pool of 364 popular anime characters across 83 series, ranked by fan popularity so they are recognisable rather than obscure.

Does it save my streak?+

Yes. Your streak and stats are stored locally in your browser, no account needed. Play daily to keep your streak alive.

How is this different from a normal anime trivia quiz?+

Instead of multiple-choice trivia, it is a visual guessing game: you identify a character from a progressively revealed image, Wordle-style, with one shared puzzle per day and a shareable result.
