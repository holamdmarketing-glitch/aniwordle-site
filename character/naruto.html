Guess the Naruto Character

A blurred Naruto character is revealed bit by bit, name them before you run out of guesses. A new Naruto character every day, with hints along the way. Free, no sign-up.

The daily Naruto character quiz

Think you know Naruto inside out? This is the Naruto character quiz for true fans: a portrait starts blurred and clears with every wrong guess, while hints unlock, gender, debut year, then role. You have six tries to name the daily character, and everyone plays the same puzzle so you can compare results.

The game features 12 of the most popular Naruto characters, including Itachi Uchiha, Kakashi Hatake, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha, Shikamaru Nara, Gaaraand more. When you run out of daily rounds, switch to practice mode for unlimited random Naruto characters.

Tips

  • Hair colour and silhouette are often visible even through the blur, start there.
  • Use the debut-year hint to place the character within the Naruto timeline.
  • Stuck? Practice mode is the fastest way to learn the full Naruto roster.

Frequently asked questions

How do you play Guess the Naruto Character?+

A blurred Naruto character appears. Type a name and pick from the suggestions (limited to Naruto characters). Each wrong guess unblurs the image and reveals a hint, gender, debut year, then role. You get six guesses.

Is there a new Naruto character every day?+

Yes. Everyone gets the same Naruto character each day, chosen deterministically by date, and it resets at midnight UTC. Practice mode lets you play unlimited random Naruto characters anytime.

Which Naruto characters can appear?+

12 of the most popular Naruto characters, including Itachi Uchiha, Kakashi Hatake, Naruto Uzumaki, Sasuke Uchiha and more, ranked by fan popularity so they are recognisable.

Is it free and do I need an account?+

It is completely free with no account needed. Your streak and stats are saved locally in your browser.
