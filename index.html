The daily anime quiz for real fans

Aniwordle is a free anime quiz built around one simple idea: a fresh puzzle every single day. Instead of a long multiple-choice test, each game is a fast, visualanime guessing game. Name the character from a blurred image, or deduce the daily answer from attribute clues. Everyone plays the same puzzle, so you can share your result and see how your anime knowledge stacks up against your friends.

Whether you grew up on shounen classics or you follow this season's airing list, there is a round for you. Puzzles are drawn from 364 popular characters across 83anime series, ranked by fan popularity so every answer is one you actually recognise.

Five ways to play

  • Guess the Anime Character: the classic anime character quiz. A portrait starts blurred and clears with every wrong guess while hints unlock. Six tries to name them.
  • Guess the Anime: identify the series from one pixelated scene that sharpens with every miss. Six tries, with genre and year hints along the way.
  • Anime Wordle (Animedle): a Wordle-style anime quiz. Guess characters and read the colour-coded grid of clues until you find today's mystery answer.
  • Higher or Lower: an endless popularity game. Decide whether the next anime character has more or fewer favourites and build the longest streak you can.
  • Anime Connections: a daily grouping puzzle. Sort sixteen characters into the four anime they come from before you run out of mistakes.

Free, daily, and no sign-up

Every anime quiz on Aniwordle is free to play and runs right in your browser. There is no account to create: your streak, stats and daily progress are saved locally on your device. Come back each day to keep your streak going, or jump into practice mode for unlimited rounds whenever you want more.

Anime quiz: frequently asked questions

What is Aniwordle?+

Aniwordle is a collection of free daily anime quiz games. Identify a mystery anime character from a blurred image, or play the anime wordle where each guess reveals matching attributes. A new puzzle drops every day, no sign-up required.

How do you play an anime quiz here?+

Pick a game, look at the clue (a blurred character image, or an attribute grid), then type a character name and choose from the suggestions. Guess the Character gives you six tries with image hints, and the Anime Wordle gives you six tries with attribute feedback.

Is there a new anime quiz every day?+

Yes. Every game has a fresh daily puzzle that is the same for all players and resets at midnight UTC, so you can compare results and build a streak. Practice mode offers unlimited random rounds anytime.

What is an anime wordle (animedle)?+

An anime wordle, also called animedle, is a Wordle-style guessing game where you identify a mystery anime character. After each guess, a grid shows how that character compares to the answer across anime, gender, role, genres, debut year and popularity, until every cell turns green.

Are the anime quiz games free?+

Completely free, with no account needed. Puzzles draw from a curated pool of 364 popular characters across 83 anime, chosen by fan popularity so they are recognisable. Your streak and stats are saved in your browser.
