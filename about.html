About Aniwordle

Aniwordle is a free collection of daily anime guessing games for fans who love testing their knowledge. Each game gives you one shared puzzle a day, the same for every player, so you can compare results and keep a streak going.

The games run entirely in your browser. There is no account to create; your streak and stats are stored locally on your device. We currently offerGuess the Characterand theAnime Wordle, with more on the way.

Where the data comes from

Puzzles draw from a curated pool of 364 popular characters across 83anime, ranked by fan popularity so every answer is recognisable. Character information and artwork are sourced from public anime databases (AniList) for editorial and fan use, and remain the property of their respective rights holders. If you are a rights holder and have a concern, please get in touch.
